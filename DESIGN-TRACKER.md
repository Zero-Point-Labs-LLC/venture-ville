## 🧩 Design Principles (Locked)

1. **Dominance over realism** — Strategic leverage matters more than simulation
2. **Asymmetry with parity** — Unique paths, equal timing
3. **Shipping slope** — Upgrades must improve economic output
4. **Shared archetypes** — Unique trees built from common building blocks
5. **Runway pressure** — Funding creates macro pacing tension
