## [vCurrent] - 2026-03-22

### Core Systems Defined

**Game Fantasy:**
- Startup competition framework
- Users + Money victory dual-track
- Asymmetric engine building

**Resource System:**
- Engineering, Design, Product, Marketing, Hiring

**Talent System:**
- Partner → Pod → Team progression
- Manager, Contractor, Influencer variants

**Roadmap Trees:**
- Company-specific upgrade paths
- Multiple viable branches
- Platform convergence design

**Progress Cards:**
- 5 discipline decks (Engineering, Design, Product, Marketing, Recruiting)
- Cities & Knights–inspired mechanics
- Tactical swingy effects

**Advancement Tracks:**
- Product: Market Fit → Moat → Monopoly
- Engineering: Algorithm → Invention → Patent
- Design: TBD

**Funding Timeline:**
- Seed → Series A → Series B → Series C → IPO
- Structural power progression

**Market Events:**
- 7-roll replacement system
- Time advancement + event trigger
- 50% Month End / 25% Tailwinds / 25% Headwinds

**Organizational Capacity:**
- Reorg mechanic (city walls analog)
- Growth vs stability tension

### Design Issues Identified

1. **Economic slope of shipping** — Prototypes/Apps need better economic impact
2. **Unique trees vs simplicity** — Balance custom content with shared archetypes

### Strategic Insights Locked

- Engineering rarely drives users directly
- Design + Marketing drive early growth
- Product is convergence glue
- Late game rewards platform control
- Engine timing parity critical
