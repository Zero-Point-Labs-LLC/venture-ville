## 🧩 Design Principles to Remember

1. **Dominance over realism** — Optimize for strategic depth, not simulation
2. **Asymmetry with parity** — Different paths, equal timing
3. **Shipping improves slope** — Upgrades must compound economic power
4. **Shared archetypes** — Custom trees from common pieces
5. **Runway pressure** — Funding creates macro tension

---

## ⚠️ Known Issues to Address

1. **Economic slope of shipping** — Prototypes/Apps need better impact
2. **Unique trees vs simplicity** — Balance custom vs shared systems
